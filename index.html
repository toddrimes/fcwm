About

I received many gifts while I was going through cancer treatment. They were all amazing and helpful. What stands out to me more than anything is the music. The songs and playlists gifted to me became a integral part of making it through the day. Music is something you can always have close by. It can turn you around and slip itself around your soul to make everything ok, even if it's just for a moment. Looking back I'm not sure how I would have made it with out my music.
- Allison W. Gryphon, Breast Cancer Fighter


The Fighting Cancer with Music collaboration is intended to bring comfort, escape and relief to everyone fighting cancer and those supporting them. We intend to grow and evolve with our community so please share your stories and the songs that impacted you during treatment with us. fighter@fightingcancerwithmusic.com
