About

I received many gifts while I was going through cancer treatment. They were all amazing. What stands out to me more than anything is the music. The songs and playlists gifted to me became an integral part of making it through the day. A Song is something you can always have close by. It can turn you around and slip itself around your soul to make everything ok, even if it's just for a moment. Looking back I'm not sure how I would have made it with out my music.
- Allison W. Gryphon, Breast Cancer Fighter


The Fighting Cancer with Music collaboration is intended to bring comfort, escape and relief to everyone fighting cancer and those supporting them.
